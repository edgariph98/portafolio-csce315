By having as many passionate people about the many possibilities Computer Science can offer leads to many possible contributions in many different ways.

Another focus of mine is the improvement of the enhancement and performance of a Computer Scientist, this is achieved by giving as many resources and helpful tools for the person, so building the right tools and resources is something that I am extremely passionate about and I always look to contribute in that field.
