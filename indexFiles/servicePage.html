My Service

very little access to technology and resources to learn more about tech

and that way for me was by getting involved in community service organizations
Engineers Serving the Community:
this is the organization where I was able to fully engage myself in the world of science and engineering
as to where most of projects are meant to improve or address a problem in our Student Community,
and where I am also to participate in different activities
to encourage younger generations into the world of Tech and Science.
